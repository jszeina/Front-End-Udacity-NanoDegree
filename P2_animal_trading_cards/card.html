Panda

Pandas have very strong jaws and the largest molar teeth of any mammal. They use these teeth to crush tough bamboo stems.

  • Scientific Name: UAiluropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: Average is 5 to 6 feet.
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Favourite Food: bamboo

The unique panda facts is that the bears do not hibernate. Their main food supply is available all year around. However they may head to lower elevations during the cold season.
